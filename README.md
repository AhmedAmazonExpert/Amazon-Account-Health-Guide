- Key metrics to track daily
- Policy violation prevention
- Performance notification response workflows
- Appeal letter frameworks

### Common Issues & Resolution
- Listing suppression troubleshooting
- Brand registry issues
- Category ungating process
- Intellectual property complaints
- A-to-Z claim management

### Customer Service SOPs
- Response templates for common inquiries
- Refund/return decision framework
- Negative review response strategy
- Buyer-seller messaging best practices

## Why Account Health Matters
A healthy account means better Buy Box share, higher organic 
ranking, and protection against suspension.
